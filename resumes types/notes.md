⚡ Simple Decision Guide (for you)
Applying to jobs in Canada → Combination + Targeted
Career fair → Mini resume + QR
Switching roles → Functional (sometimes)
Stable experience → Chronological
⚠️ Honest advice (no sugarcoating)

If you send a pure functional resume, some recruiters in Canada will:
👉 think you’re hiding experience
👉 skip it

So don’t rely on it alone.

🔥 What you should do

For your situation (developer, projects, newcomer):

👉 Main CV: Combination
👉 For each job: tweak (Targeted)
👉 For networking: QR + mini resume (you already had a good idea)
