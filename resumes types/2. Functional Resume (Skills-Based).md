Structure:
Focus on skills → then small section for experience

Use it when:

You’re changing careers
You’re a fresh graduate
You have gaps in employment
You want to highlight skills over job titles

Example for you:
Instead of:

“PHP Developer at X”

You show:

Backend Development: Laravel, APIs, authentication
Frontend: Tailwind, React
Projects: your systems

Avoid if:

You have strong, relevant work history (it can look like you're hiding something)
